## Risks per phase

**Week 1:** Infrastructure stalls because we wait for "perfect." Mitigation: 60-90 min/day cap. If it's not done in time, ship rougher.

**Week 2:** First reel flops, momentum dies. Mitigation: 5 reels in week 2, not 1. Spread risk.

**Weeks 3-6:** Hook fatigue, energy drops. Mitigation: batch-record Fridays so daily posting is just scheduling, not creation.

**Weeks 7-8:** Paid offer underwhelms. Mitigation: founding member price + 100x community already has 200+ members by then = social proof.

**Week 9+:** Burnout. Mitigation: paid tier funds either an editor or a VA so Brendan only does the on-camera work + 30 min of community presence.
